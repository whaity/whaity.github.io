What is Zero Trust

In the old days, there was a port on your firewall, sometimes yellow, that was marked untrusted, and another marked trusted. Anything that was plugged in to the trusted port was trusted. The believe was that only bad people were on the untrusted side. This was not a new consept. It is basically the same idea behind castles and forts. The walls would protect the defenders of the castle. Your firewall was the walls of the castle. Well you don’t need to work long in modern IT to start to see the problems with this.
